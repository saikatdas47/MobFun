Farm Village Life

I assumed you meant a Stardew Valley-style village game.
Walk around, till soil, plant seeds, water crops, harvest, sell, and sleep to pass days.

Left joystick = move.
ACT = use selected tool on nearby tile.
TOOL = switch Hoe / Seeds / Water / Harvest.
Desktop: WASD or arrows to move, Space to act, T to switch tool.

How To Play

1. Go near brown ground tiles in the farm area.
2. Use Hoe to till soil.
3. Switch to Seeds and plant.
4. Switch to Water and water daily.
5. After a few days crops become ready.
6. Use Harvest to collect and earn coins.
7. Go to the house door and sleep to start a new day.
